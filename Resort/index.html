Welcome to our beautiful Tropical bay resort

Tropical Bay Resort

Tropical bay resort is situated in the quiet southern part of the Tropical bay resort,Barbados close to the Barbados Sands resort and 17 km from the capital. It offers a large outdoor pool, and a spa area with an indoor pool. A free parasol and sunbeds at the pool or the beach is provided. Tropical bay resort is set in a picturesque woodland location, overlooking the beach. Tropical bay resort features a main restaurant and a lobby bar. Rent-a-car services and bike hire are provided. The area offers several sport activities such as tennis and bicycles. The bus station, offering regular transport to the capital, is set 100 m away. The centre of Tropical bay resort can be reached within a 15-minute stroll.
This is our guests' favourite part of Barbados, according to independent reviews.
